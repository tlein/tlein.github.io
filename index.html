My name is Tucker Lein, I am a Brooklyn-based Software Developer at Rockstar Games. During my free time, I enjoy experimenting in graphics programming and drawing. Before starting at Rockstar Games, I helped create a suite of open source gamedev projects which you can find on my github.

In 2018, I co-founded ILD Games LLC and released the mobile game Squatbot (iOS and Android) using the open source gamedev projects mentioned above.
