Squatbot

iOS & Android Game

Squatbot is a mobile platformer released for iOS and Android. This is a unique two-button platformer designed for mobile controls in mind. Squatbot is developed on top of the Ancona game engine and SFML using C++11.

Squatbot was ILD Games LLC’s first release, and I was the lead programmer on the game. Primary responsibilities included:

  • General gameplay programming
  • Lead other programmers for their tasks
  • Porting engine and game to iOS
  • Menu programming
  • Integrating 3rd party services for social features
  • Developing business relationships with 3rd party services
